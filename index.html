01 — Approach

A focused practice.
Measured counsel.

We represent businesses across a wide range of industries and sizes — from formation and financing through operations, transactions, and expansion. Alongside traditional legal representation, we provide strategic advisory counsel to executives and leadership teams on matters that span legal, business, and market dimensions.

The work centers on avoiding problems through careful planning and well-drafted contracts, and on resolving them efficiently when they arise. We evaluate outcomes, educate clients to support informed decisions, and build strategies around the client's actual goals — not the ones lawyers are often tempted to substitute.

When a matter calls for specialized expertise, we collaborate with outside consultants, accountants, and co-counsel. We consider the client relationship itself to be part of the work product.

02 — Services

Practice areas.

I. Advisory

Strategic Advisory & Consulting

Counsel to senior leadership on corporate strategy, communications, regulatory positioning, and commercial initiatives. Engagements range from discrete advisory projects to ongoing roles supporting executive teams through complex transitions and market shifts.

II. Counsel

Outside General Counsel

Day-to-day legal support for companies without in-house counsel — vendor and customer agreements, employment documentation, compliance review, and the routine matters that keep a business moving forward.

III. Corporate

Corporate & Business

Entity formation, governance, shareholder and operating agreements, mergers and acquisitions, commercial lending, contract drafting and negotiation, and general business counsel across the corporate lifecycle.

IV. Real Estate

Commercial Real Estate

Purchase and sale, leasing, financing, 1031 exchanges, development matters, and real estate holdings structuring — drawing on years of in-house operating experience.

03 — Attorney

C. Todd Cammack.

C. Todd Cammack, Esq.

Todd Cammack practices corporate, business, and real estate law from Raleigh, North Carolina, representing owners and closely-held companies across the state.

He began his career as a finance attorney at Parker Poe in Raleigh, then left traditional practice to co-found a Raleigh-based commercial real estate company — an operator's perspective he now brings back to the counsel side of the table. The combination informs a practice oriented toward practical outcomes rather than procedural theater.

Todd graduated with honors from North Carolina State University in 1998 with a degree in Business Administration (magna cum laude), and received his Juris Doctor from the Wake Forest University School of Law in 2001.
